We are a small french indie studio making narrative and mechanic heavy games for art lovers



Stack Order is an indie game combining boss battles, deckbuilding, and roguelite elements, with a strong narrative focus.

Presented in a 2D comic-inspired art style, the player alternates between boss fights, exploration phases, dialogues with NPCs, and comic-style sequences that expand the story.

The core gameplay is built around a card-stacking system: players layer cards with different effects to create powerful combos and carefully planned attacks.
